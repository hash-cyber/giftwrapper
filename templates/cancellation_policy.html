Cancellation Policy

At GiftWrapper, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. This Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which orders may be cancelled and refunded.

1. Order Cancellation

Orders cannot be cancelled once placed, as processing begins immediately. However, in exceptional cases (such as accidental orders), customers may contact our support team, and requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis without guarantee.

2. Customized Hampers

Customized or personalized hampers are specially prepared based on customer requirements. Once the customization process has started, such orders cannot be cancelled or modified.

3. Cancellation by GiftWrapper

We reserve the right to cancel any order due to reasons such as product unavailability, pricing errors, payment issues, or suspected fraudulent activity. In such cases, a full refund will be issued.

4. Refund Policy

If an order is successfully cancelled, refunds will be processed within 3–5 business days to the original payment method. The exact time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. Late Cancellation Requests

Cancellation requests made after the allowed time window may not be accepted. However, customers may contact support for assistance, and requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

6. Non-Cancellable Orders

Orders that are already shipped, out for delivery, or delivered cannot be cancelled. In such cases, customers may refer to our Return & Refund Policy if applicable.

7. How to Request Cancellation

Customers can request order cancellation through their account dashboard or by contacting customer support with order details.

8. Contact Support

If you need assistance with cancelling an order or have any questions, please contact our support team through the website.
